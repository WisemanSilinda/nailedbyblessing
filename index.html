About Me

Hi, I'm Blessing

Welcome to Nailed By Blessing, where your nail dreams come to life. With years of experience in the nail industry, I've perfected the art of creating beautiful, long-lasting nail enhancements that complement your unique style.

My passion for nail artistry drives me to stay updated with the latest trends and techniques, ensuring you receive top-quality service every time you visit.

Our Services

Manicure Services

Complete nail care including expert shaping, gentle cuticle care, and flawless polish application. Our manicure treatments are designed to nourish your nails and hands, leaving them healthy, smooth, and beautifully polished. Choose from a variety of colors and designs to express your unique style.

Pedicure Services

Revitalize your feet with our luxurious pedicure treatments that include soothing foot baths, exfoliation, nail trimming, and moisturizing massages. Perfect for relieving tired feet and improving skin texture, our pedicures leave you feeling refreshed and confident to step out in style.

Additional Services

Complement your nail care with our range of additional services including intricate nail art, chrome finishes, gel overlays, and nail repairs. Whether you want to add sparkle, texture, or extra durability, our skilled technicians will customize your look to perfection.
